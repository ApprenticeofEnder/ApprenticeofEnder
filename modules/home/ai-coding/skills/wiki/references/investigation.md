# Investigation template

A record of a debugging effort, root-cause analysis, or research dig: how the conclusion was
reached, not just the conclusion. **Publish format: Markdown** (`contentFormat: "markdown"`).

Fill the sections below; drop any that don't apply.

```md
## Summary

One or two sentences: what was investigated and the headline finding.

## Trigger

What prompted this — the alert, bug report, question, or symptom.

## Timeline of findings

Dated, chronological notes of what was checked and what it showed.

- **YYYY-MM-DD** — observation / step taken → result

## Evidence

Concrete artifacts: logs, metrics, repro steps, queries, links.

## Root cause

The underlying cause the evidence supports.

## Hypotheses considered & ruled out

- Hypothesis → why it was ruled out.

## Recommendations / next steps

- Action — owner — priority.

## Open questions

Anything still unresolved.
```
